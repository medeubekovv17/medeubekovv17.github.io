TULPAR.KZ shygu tarihy

Sait "TULPAR.KZ" 1996 jyldyñ tamyzynan 2014 jyldyñ jeltoqsanyna deiın Qazaqstanda şyğarylğan attas gazet negızınde qūryldy. Tabysty qyzmet etken jyldary ol auqymdy derbes jobağa ainaldy. Būl tek onlain-tegın habarlandyru taqtasy ğana emes, sonymen qatar avtokölık jürgızushılerı men olar boludy josparlap otyrğandar üşın naqty naryq jäne elektrondy nūsqaulyq.

Qoldanu joly qarapaiym, mumkindigi kop bul saittyn basshylary Nurlan Kulmurzayev, Azamat Talap, Sergazy Medeubekov, Gaziz Aibaruly
